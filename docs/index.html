Amartya Dutta

    Hey there! My name is Amartya Dutta and I am from the beautiful city of Guwahati in the state of Assam, India. I am a Graduate Student in the Department of Computer Science at Virginia Tech. I am presently a Graduate Research Assistant at the Knowledge Guided Machine Learning (KGML) Lab at Virginia Tech where I work under the guidance of my advisor Dr. Anuj Karpatne. I received my Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering from the Indian Institute of Information Technology Guwahati where I worked with Dr. Ferdous Ahmed Barbhuiya. I am also a a graduate student researcher working with the Imageomics Institute.

    My research interests are in Deep Learning and Computer Vision. I am also interested in interdisciplinary applications of AI. My research currently focuses on Image Segmentation and incorporating Knowledge Graphs into driving the segmentation process. I have previously also worked on Augmented and Virtual Reality and have built applications based around them using Unity.
